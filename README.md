Customer Retention & Churn Analysis using Power BI

Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important factors for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer churn behavior using the Telco Customer Churn dataset and provides actionable insights to help businesses improve customer retention and reduce churn.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Power BI and focuses on identifying churn patterns, customer segments at risk, and factors influencing customer retention.



Objectives

* Analyze customer churn and retention trends.
* Calculate overall churn rate.
* Identify customer segments with high churn risk.
* Study customer lifetime patterns using tenure data.
* Generate business recommendations to improve customer retention.

Dataset:Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, subscription details, service usage, contract information, payment methods, monthly charges, total charges, tenure, and churn status.

Key Findings

1. Contract Type Impact

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts show significantly higher churn compared to customers with One-Year and Two-Year contracts.

2. Payment Method Impact

Customers using Electronic Check exhibit higher churn rates than customers using automatic payment methods.

3. Internet Service Impact

Fiber Optic customers are more likely to churn compared to DSL customers.

4. Customer Tenure

Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave the service, while long-term customers demonstrate better retention.

5. Customer Demographics

Gender has minimal impact on churn, while Senior Citizen customers show slightly higher churn behavior.
